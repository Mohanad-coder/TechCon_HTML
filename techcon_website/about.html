Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate technology enthusiasts who envisioned creating a platform where innovation meets collaboration. What started as a small gathering of 200 tech professionals in a community center has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from more than 50 countries.

Over the past fourteen years, TechCon has been at the forefront of technological advancement, hosting discussions on emerging technologies before they became mainstream. From the early days of cloud computing to today's AI revolution, TechCon has consistently provided a stage for groundbreaking ideas and innovations that have shaped the tech industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the global technology community. We believe that innovation thrives when diverse minds come together to share knowledge, challenge conventions, and collaborate on solutions to the world's most pressing technological challenges.

We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, and technology enthusiasts can learn from industry leaders, discover cutting-edge technologies, and build meaningful connections that extend far beyond the conference halls. Our goal is to empower every attendee to return to their communities as catalysts for positive technological change.

Through our carefully curated sessions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities, we strive to create experiences that not only inform but transform the way people think about and engage with technology.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has been honored to host some of the brightest minds in technology. Our speakers have included pioneering entrepreneurs, leading researchers, and visionary leaders who have shaped the digital landscape.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Dr. Sarah Chen, Chief AI Officer at TechVision Labs, delivered a groundbreaking keynote at TechCon 2022 on the ethical implications of artificial intelligence. Her work in developing responsible AI frameworks has influenced policy discussions worldwide. With over 15 years of experience in machine learning and neural networks, Dr. Chen has published more than 50 research papers and holds several patents in AI technology.

Marcus Rodriguez

Marcus Rodriguez, renowned cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet Solutions, shared his insights on protecting critical infrastructure at TechCon 2021. His presentation on quantum-resistant encryption methods has become one of the most referenced talks in the cybersecurity community. Marcus has advised governments and Fortune 500 companies on cybersecurity strategy and has been instrumental in preventing several major cyber attacks.

Priya Patel

Priya Patel, Lead Software Architect at OpenCode Foundation, inspired audiences at TechCon 2020 with her presentation on the power of open-source collaboration. Her contributions to major open-source projects have impacted millions of developers worldwide. Priya is a strong advocate for diversity in tech and has mentored hundreds of women entering the software development field through her nonprofit initiative, CodeHer.

James Okonkwo

James Okonkwo, blockchain innovator and CEO of ChainTech Innovations, presented a compelling vision for decentralized systems at TechCon 2023. His work in developing sustainable blockchain solutions has earned him recognition as one of the top 30 entrepreneurs under 30. James has successfully launched three blockchain-based platforms that serve millions of users across Africa and beyond, revolutionizing financial inclusion and digital identity management.
